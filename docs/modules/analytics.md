# Analytics Module

Analytics provides business performance visibility across revenue, order volume, and catalog behavior.

## Included Dashboards

- Sales overview by period
- Product performance
- Category performance
- Revenue metrics summary

## Period Controls

Admins can switch between:

- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Yearly

Additional controls include year/month selectors for segmented analysis.

## Revenue Metrics Cards

- Today Revenue
- Monthly Revenue (+growth indicator)
- Yearly Revenue
- Average Order Value

## Utility Actions

- `Refresh` to sync latest analytics payload.
- `Generate Sample Data` for development/demo environments.

## Recommended Reporting Routine

1. Review monthly revenue and growth delta.
2. Identify low-performing categories.
3. Cross-check with Orders and Promo Codes for campaign effects.
4. Adjust catalog and campaign strategy.
